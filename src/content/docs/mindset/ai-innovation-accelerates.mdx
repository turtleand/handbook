---
title: Your Assumptions Expire Fast
description: AI capabilities change faster than your beliefs about them. What failed last month might work today.
---

AI capabilities change [faster than your assumptions about them](https://growth.turtleand.com/posts/check-again/). The task you dismissed as "AI can't do that" three months ago? Try it again. The workflow you built around a limitation? That limitation might be gone.

The most valuable information is what just happened, because it hasn't been priced in yet. Old information isn't worthless. It's just not urgent. Don't let it compete with what's changing right now.
